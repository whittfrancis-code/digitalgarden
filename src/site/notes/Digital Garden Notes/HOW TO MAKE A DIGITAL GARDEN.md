There's a million different ways you can go about this, but this is what worked best for my use case

&nbsp;
### Requirements

&nbsp;

- GitHub Account
- Vercel Account
- Obsidian
- Digital Garden Plugin for Obsidian
- 2 functioning braincells that can be used to think and follow instructions on the internet

&nbsp;

Steps:

&nbsp;

1. Fork this repo: https://github.com/oleeskild/digitalgarden
2. Deploy to your Vercel
3. Publish to the Internet
4. Create an access token on your repo: https://github.com/settings/personal-access-tokens/new
5. Fill out GitHub info in the Digital Garden Plugin Settings inside of Obsidian
6. Publish your first note
7. ???
8. Profit!
